Welcome to the Family Site

This will be our family hub where we can stay up to date on Birthdays, Holidays, and individual family member information as well as photos.

How to Use This Site:

  • Simply click on the Events page to see a list of events that are coming up within the family.
  • Simply click on an idividual's profile image to see their profile page. Listed on the profile pages will be contact and address information, as well as a list of photos and fun facts about that family member.
  • Another page you'll see listed at the top is the Profile Form. Clicking on this will take you to the Profile Form page where you can submit information for your profile page. You can submit multiple entries but just make sure to leave your name every time with each entry.
  • You'll also see a suggestions box toward the bottom of the home page. Feel free to leave any suggestions you feel would help improve the site.
  • Also one last thing to note. If you ever see "lorem ipsum" anywhere on the site, that is just filler text before real content is added.
